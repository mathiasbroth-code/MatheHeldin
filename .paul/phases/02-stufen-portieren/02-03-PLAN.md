<objective>
## Goal
Port 5 stages: 3 Legen-Stages (shared ZahlLegenView) + Buendeln + Zerlegen (each with standalone View). After this plan, 10 of 11 stages are playable.

## Purpose
Legen-Stages are the counterpart to Lesen: instead of reading blocks, children build numbers with +/- counters. Buendeln and Zerlegen train place-value understanding — core skills for the Fredo curriculum.

## Output
- `src/stages/shared/ZahlLegenView.tsx` — shared View for Legen (target → +/- counters → auto-check)
- `src/stages/buendeln/BuendelnView.tsx` — standalone (Einer-Grid → Zehner/Einer input)
- `src/stages/zerlegen/ZerlegenView.tsx` — standalone (Zahl → T/H/Z/E inputs)
- 5 stage modules registered (total: 10 stages)
</objective>
