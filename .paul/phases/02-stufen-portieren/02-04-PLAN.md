## Goal
Port the Skizze stage — the most complex exercise with a 3-phase flow: (1) order stations on a number line, (2) enter known distances, (3) calculate the unknown distance. This completes Phase 2 with all 11 stages.

## Purpose
Skizze trains systematic problem-solving for word problems — a core Fredo-4 skill. The 3-phase structure (per CLAUDE.md didactic rules) scaffolds the child through: understand → sketch → solve. This is the only stage that uses the `phases/` sub-directory pattern.

- 11th stage registered in registry (total: 11)
